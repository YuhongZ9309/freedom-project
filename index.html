3rd-parties VPN providers might not be has secure as you think it is.

Free VPN does not mean it is “Risk-Free”. Many companies use VPN to share their file securely and anonymously. Often time they take your information and sell it, that is how they make money. Other time providers are often have shady stuff going on, such as injecting JavaScript in the users’ web browsers for advertising and tracking.

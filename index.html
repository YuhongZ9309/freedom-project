What is a VPN?

A VPN, Virtual Private Network, is a service where you can access the internet safely by routing your connection to a server and hiding your activities. VPN are commonly use to secure connections to public WiFi, acccess restircted website, and shielding your privacy.

How

How is it protective

When you are connected to a VPN and your data is encrypted, others will only see the data encrypted and they will have to decrypt the data, which can be very time-consuming or impossible.

How does it work

The VPN client encrypts the query or data the user enters. From there, the encrypted data goes to the user’s Internet Service Provider (ISP) and then to the VPN server. The server then connects to the public Internet, pointing the user to the query results or banking website.

How VPN overall is benefical

VPN help protect what you want to hide from other that you don't want to see. There no harms or draw back from having a VPN aside from certain one that sells your information to make money, but most of them are generally not. Making your own VPN is more safe for you and your information.

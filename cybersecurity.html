What is Hacking?

Hacking is referred to seek information through your mechanical devices like your smartphones, laptop, and computers. The term “hacker” has a negative connotation towards it and is considered as malicious, but the term isn’t all negative. Nowadays, they are mostly considered as bad people who want your information, but most of them are doing this because they want to seek a challenge.
